The Himalayas

Asia

The world's highest mountains, offering breathtaking views, challenging treks, and serene monasteries. A true adventure destination.

Best Time to Visit: March to May or September to November.

Key Attraction: Mount Everest Base Camp Trek.

Required Gear: Thermal wear, sturdy hiking boots.

Great Barrier Reef

Australia

The world's largest coral reef system, home to diverse marine life. Perfect for snorkeling, diving, and boat tours.

Best Time to Visit: June to October (dry season).

Key Attraction: Scuba diving amongst the colorful coral.

Required Gear: Sunscreen (reef safe), dive mask.

Machu Picchu

Peru, South America

An ancient Incan citadel set high in the Andes Mountains. A UNESCO World Heritage site and a testament to Incan engineering.

Best Time to Visit: April to October (dry season).

Key Attraction: The classic Inca Trail hike.

Required Gear: Altitude sickness medication, layered clothing.
